# Expression Helpers

### .OR, .AND, .XOR, .NOT
The above directives are equivalents of the logical operators `||` (`.OR`), `&&` (`.AND`), `^` (`.XOR`), and `!` (`.NOT`).


<a name="lohi"></a>

### .LO (expression), .HI (expression)
The above directives are equivalents of the operators `<` (low byte) and `>` (high byte) respectively.
